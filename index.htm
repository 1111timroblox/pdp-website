Suburban housing, school zone (20 mph), park. Watch for speeders during school hours.

TRAFFIC STOP ‚Äì 7 STEP

  1. Select safe location, signal driver to curb.
  2. Run plate & warrant check before exit.
  3. Approach left-rear, greet & identify.
  4. State reason for stop.
  5. Request docs (license, registration).
  6. Use discretion: cite, warn, or arrest.
  7. Clear scene, log in CAD.
